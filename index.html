About Us

Company Overview

DREAMSKY SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY LIMITED is a mobile internet marketing company founded in 2017, dedicated to providing global marketing and promotion services, helping clients efficiently acquire users, enhance brand awareness, and achieve commercial monetization.

Industry Experience

With outstanding service capabilities and a strong industry reputation, the company has accumulated diverse internet media resources and operational advantages, providing comprehensive and professional overseas marketing solutions for hundreds of advertiser clients across cross-border e-commerce, utility apps, entertainment, finance, and other industries.

Professional Positioning

As a "Marketing Service & Consulting Expert," we provide comprehensive mobile internet marketing services from multiple perspectives, offering professional marketing consulting, advertising, training, and other consulting services. Our primary target markets include Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, Europe, the Americas, and Southeast Asia.

As a global mobile internet marketing service provider, the company actively expands full-case marketing in vertical sectors on top of its existing internet advertising services. Leveraging deep understanding of overseas markets and extensive experience in international promotion, we have built an integrated online-offline marketing service ecosystem, delivering one-stop, comprehensive marketing solutions for our advertiser clients.
